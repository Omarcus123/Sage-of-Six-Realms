Who Should I Play?

Play whoever looks cool or fun to you! Learning is much more fun when you enjoy the character. Try multiple characters until you find one that fits your style.

For competitive players, you may want to pick a top-tier character that fits your playstyle, but don't feel forced to. Many players still do well with off-meta picks.
